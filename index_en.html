About me

Santista at heart!

Born and raised in Santos, on the coast of São Paulo, I'm passionate about the beach, sports, and everything that involves creativity.

My design journey began in 2015, with courses at Microcamp, where I had my first contact with tools like Adobe Dreamweaver and Flash.

In 2017, I deepened my knowledge in graphic design, video editing, and motion, with Adobe Premiere and After Effects, at Evolute school.

Still in 2017, I began my studies in UX/UI Design through online courses, which led me to my first professional opportunity in the area, working with UX/UI and front-end.

Since then, I have more than 8 years of experience in technology and design, always combining strategy, creativity, and user focus.

I believe that collaboration and good communication are fundamental to creating efficient and sustainable solutions.

I like to work side by side with different teams, understanding the real needs of the user and contributing to strategic decisions that avoid rework and generate business value.
